                            OBJECTIVE
---------------------------------------------------------------------
    Be the first to crack the randomly-generated, 4-digit code.

    Leaderboard rankings are based on time elapsed during guesses.
    Time for loading screens and feedback aren't counted.

    Score is best of 15, with points deducted for each attempt after
    the first, and each invalid attempt.

                            HINT GUIDE
---------------------------------------------------------------------
    x = correct number in a correct position.

    o = correct number(s) in an incorrect position.

    * = incorrect number.
